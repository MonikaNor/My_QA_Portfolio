2. Financefy.ca Project (FCP)

The objective of this test project is to assess the reliability and accessibility of Financefy.ca's primary features, ensuring they meet quality standards for functionality, usability, and compatibility. Testing environment: Windows 11 Home, Version 22H2, Display Resolution 1920 x 1080, Browser Google Chrome (64-bit).
